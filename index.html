Loop's Magic

The straight-forward definition of Loop is a Hi-Tech academy. Its purpose is to expose the next generations (called Loopers) to the world of computer science and technology and offer them a personalized fun-based educational method according to each student's preferences and ambitions in a well-structured and modular customized approach. Also, since it is a long-term program, Loop is built to guide the Loopers through the years.
